How Story Mode Works

Four simple steps to create a professional video story

1

Add Videos to Studio

Click 'Add to Studio' on Grok favorites page, or drag and drop local videos into the Side Panel.

2

Arrange Your Story

Drag and drop segments in the Storyboard to reorder. The first video becomes the Master that defines output specs.

3

Generate Continuations

Select a segment, browse the filmstrip to pick any frame, enter a prompt, and send to Grok. Frame Lock ensures seamless continuation.

4

Merge & Export

When all segments are ready, click export. FFmpeg will merge all videos locally and download the result.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Story Mode

What is Story Mode in GrokMediaDownloader?

Story Mode lets you compile your downloaded Grok AI images into a scrollable visual story or slideshow. Instead of a flat folder of images, you can arrange them in a meaningful sequence, preview the flow, and share or export the whole story as a single file. It is ideal for creators who generate image series or visual narratives with Grok.

How do I create a story from my Grok images?

Open the Story Mode panel in the extension, select images from your collection, arrange them in the desired order by dragging and dropping, then click "Create Story." The story is instantly generated and ready to preview or export.

Can I customize the order of images in my story?

Yes. Story Mode includes a drag-and-drop reordering interface. You can freely rearrange images before finalizing the story. The order you set is preserved in the exported output.

How many images can I include in a single story?

Story Mode supports up to several hundred images per story. For best performance we recommend keeping stories under 100 images, but there is no hard technical limit imposed by the extension itself.

What export formats does Story Mode support?

Story Mode can export your image sequence as a ZIP archive of the ordered images, or as a self-contained HTML slideshow file that can be opened in any browser. More export formats may be added in future PRO updates.

Can I add captions or titles to images in my story?

Yes. Story Mode allows you to add a text caption to each image. Captions are displayed below the image in the story view and are included in the exported HTML slideshow, making it easy to add context or narration to each frame.
