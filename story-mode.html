Core Features

Visual Storyboard

Drag and drop video segments for intuitive timeline management. Add videos from Grok favorites with one click.

Frame Extraction

Automatically extract first and last frames from each video for quick content preview and identification.

Local FFmpeg Merge

How Story Mode Works

Four simple steps to create a professional video story

1

Add Videos to Studio

Click 'Add to Studio' on Grok favorites page, or drag and drop local videos into the Side Panel.

2

Arrange Your Story

Drag and drop segments in the Storyboard to reorder. The first video becomes the Master that defines output specs.

3

Generate Continuations

Select a segment to view its last frame, enter a prompt, and send to Grok to generate a continuation video.

4

Merge & Export

When all segments are ready, click export. FFmpeg will merge all videos locally and download the result.

Technical Highlights

WebAssembly FFmpeg

Chrome Side Panel API

Uses Chrome's latest Side Panel API for a persistent workspace that doesn't interfere with your main browsing.

Fully Local Processing

All video data is processed locally. Nothing is uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy for your creations.
