Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Data Collection & Usage GrokMediaDownloader is a client-side extension. We do NOT collect, store, or share any of your personal data, browsing history, or downloaded content. All processing happens locally on your device. 2. Permissions The permissions we request are strictly for core functionality: - Reading the current Grok page to detect media. - Saving media files to your computer. - Storing your preferences locally. 3. Third-Party Services Payments are processed via Lemon Squeezy. Your financial data is handled directly by Lemon Squeezy; this extension does not access sensitive financial information. 4. Updates We reserve the right to update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.

2. Permissions Explained

The permissions we request (such as "Storage", "Downloads", "Host Permissions") are used solely for the core functionality of the extension:

  • Reading the Grok page you're currently browsing to detect media.
  • Downloading files to your specified location.
  • Storing local extension settings and download history.

3. Third-Party Services

This extension uses LemonSqueezy as its payment processor. When purchasing a license, your payment information is handled by LemonSqueezy according to their privacy policy. We only receive the minimal information required for license verification (such as the license key).

4. Data Security

Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk of data breach. All your content remains on your device.

5. Policy Updates

In case of significant changes, we will notify users through extension updates. Continued use of the Service indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

6. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us via the "Contact Support" link at the bottom of the page.
